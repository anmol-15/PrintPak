Printpack Core Values

At Printpack, we deeply care for our associates, partners, and customers. One way to show that we care is by adhering to our core values.

TREAT PEOPLE RIGHT

  • Respectful – Treat all people with dignity and respect
  • Open – Be inclusive, welcoming, and approachable; support diversity in our workplace

CARE ABOUT WHAT WE DO

  • Customer Focused – Constantly seek ways to provide greater value; be driven by a desire to serve; go beyond the ordinary
  • Passionate – Approach business challenges and opportunities with infectious enthusiasm
  • Committed – Demonstrate a strong work ethic; take pride in doing a good job
  • Inspired – Seek excellence in everything we do

FOCUS ON HOW WE ACHIEVE RESULTS

  • Collaborative – Foster teamwork with a “can do” attitude; prioritize team and group goals over individual objectives
  • Entrepreneurial – Have a sense of urgency and a bias for action
  • Innovative – Challenge the process, encourage and accept change, experiment, explore the unknown, and take risks

DO WHAT WE SAY WE WILL DO

  • Trustworthy – Act with integrity, do what we say we will do, take actions consistent with our words
  • Reliable – Be responsive and fulfill whatever commitments we make
  • Accountable – Accept responsibility for the results and outcomes of our actions
